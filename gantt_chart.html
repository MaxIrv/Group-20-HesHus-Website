Weekly Ghantt Charts

For the first assessment each week two gantt charts are provided, one containing a general overview of progress, and the other expanding more deeply into the areas of each deliverable that were completed.

For the second deliverable each week only one gantt chart was provided as we felt it was more simple and sufficient to express the order tasks were completed and their progress for each week.
